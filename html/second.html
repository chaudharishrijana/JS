About EcoTips

EcoTips is a simple resource for individuals who want to live more sustainably. Our goal is to help you reduce your environmental impact with easy, everyday tips.

Top 3 Easy Eco-Friendly Tips

1. Use Reusable Bags

Plastic bags are harmful to the environment. Bring your own cloth or reusable bags when shopping.

2. Switch to LED Bulbs

LED bulbs use less energy and last longer than traditional ones. They save money and the planet!

3. Reduce Water Waste

Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth and fix leaks promptly to conserve water.
